Senior project by Victor Polisetty, Maya Harris, Ryan Circelli, Travis Maddox, and Moinak Dey

InstaGatorAI, our senior project advised by Sanjeev Koppal, aims to increase Florida Gators' fan engagement by automating the creation and sharing of AI-generated content related to sports events. By leveraging technologies such as ChatGPT, DALLÂ·E for image generation, TypeScript, and Puppeteer for web scraping, alongside React for frontend development, our team seeks to transform traditional sports schedules into engaging visual narratives. This system automatically scrapes upcoming game schedules, generates creative prompts to produce captivating images featuring participating mascots, and posts these visuals to Instagram and this website. InstaGatorAI combines advanced AI with social media automation to enhance fan experiences.
